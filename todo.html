Discover the Best of Terre Haute!

Explore Sugarcamp Creek

Enjoy the natural beauty and walking trails at Sugarcamp Creek. It's a perfect spot for a peaceful stroll or a family picnic amidst scenic surroundings.

Visit the Terre Haute Children's Museum

Ignite your child's imagination with interactive exhibits and hands-on activities. A fun and educational experience for all ages!

Wander through Downtown Terre Haute

Experience the heart of the city with its unique shops, local eateries, and historic architecture. Discover hidden gems and soak in the local culture.

Catch a Show at the Hulman Center

Check the schedule for concerts, sporting events, and performances at the Hulman Center, a popular venue for entertainment in the Wabash Valley.

Learn at the Clabber Girl Museum

Delve into the history of baking and the iconic Clabber Girl brand. A sweet treat for history buffs and baking enthusiasts alike!
