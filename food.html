Savor the Flavors of Terre Haute!

Terre Haute Brewing Company

A historic brewery offering a wide selection of craft beers and delicious pub fare in a lively atmosphere. Often known for special events that bring the city and surrounding areas together.

The Original Spaghetti & Sub House

A well known places famous for Italian classic pastas and subs and classic Italian decor.

The Copper Bar

A local bar with deep historic roots going back to 1928, The Copper Bar has been around since prohibition and was even a favorite of John Dillinger

Lemongrass Thai Restaurant

Indulge in authentic Thai dishes, from spicy curries to flavorful noodle soups, prepared with traditional recipes.
